What is an EMI Calculator?

An EMI calculator helps borrowers calculate the monthly installment required to repay a loan. It considers the loan amount, interest rate and tenure to determine the fixed monthly payment.

Benefits of Step-Up EMI

Step-up EMI allows borrowers to increase their EMI gradually every year. This is useful for individuals whose income is expected to increase over time. Increasing EMI helps reduce the total interest paid and closes the loan faster.

How Lump Sum Payments Reduce Loan Tenure

Making periodic lump sum payments directly reduces the loan principal. Since interest is calculated on the outstanding principal, reducing the balance early can significantly lower total interest and shorten the loan duration.
